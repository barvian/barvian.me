I opened a PR for [tailwindcss.com](https://tailwindcss.com) to incorporate [View Transitions](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/View_Transitions_API)
into their Algolia DocSearch modals. I used Jake Archibald's awesome [`linear()` generator](https://linear-easing-generator.netlify.app/) to emulate springs for
parts of the transition.
